Why do younger rabbits come down with bloat?

January 21st, 2022


At 5days to 16weeks young kits/weanlings have a very high appetite for food ( vegetables, hay and pellets) . If feeds are abundantly available , these young rabbits keep eating relentlessly. At this young age , the digestive system is naive and would not withstand much stress and pressure if stacked up with much feed . however the youngster compromise this detriment . Over feeding then sets in and gradually does indigestion. Eventually the digestion process is retarded and “bloat” sets in . Gradual swelling starts, the stomach continues swelling and becomes inflated with excess air .when the stomach is touched, it sounds like a drum . Bloat is hardly treatable if not at its earliest stage . They rabbits gradually loose appetite and become weaker as the situation becomes worse .the final stage is death . Feeds should be given in minimal quality to prevent this from happening and water should be available always
