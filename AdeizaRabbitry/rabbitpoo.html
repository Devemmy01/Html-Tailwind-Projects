the importance of rabbit poo

January 21st, 2022


Rabbit poo, indeed one of least talked about as far as rabbit farming is concerned. The essence of this article is to enlighten every reader about some benefits of this despised treasure.


With immerse soil depletion, toxicity and higher acidic concentration, we ask the question "what can we do?". Rabbit poo which has proven to be one of the best organic fertilizers and has come a long way to enriching the soil and improving its profile makes the difference


At Adeiza rabbitry and other major rabbit farms on the plateau, we have used this valuable manure to grow fruit crops, veggies and annual crops such as maize (corn) and with no doubt, the results have been tremendous.


Go beyond pelt (rabbit skin) and meat, try the organic rabbit manure and yoou will be glad you did.
