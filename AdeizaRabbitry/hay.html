the importance of hay in rabbit

January 21st, 2022


Hay in simple terms can be defined as grass cut, dried and used as food for animals.


The importance of hay in rabbit farming cannot be overemphasized as it plays an importance role in every aspect of the business.

In modern feeding charts for rabbits, it shows that hay should be given in higher amount(about 80%) than even concentrates or pellets. The contrary in the case of most farmers as most farmers tend to bombard their rabbits with concentrates and pellets which if not checked, leads to digestive problems like boat in young rabbits (Kits / warrens).


Rabbits which are hindgut fermenters require hay as a basic part of diet and nutrition. Apart from nutritive importance of hay , there comes another significant role which is the aspect of nesting materials. Hay is used along side fur to prepare nesting boxes for kindling by a female rabbits(s) (Doe). These listed roles above are few out of many of reasons why you should hay.
Give hay today, make rabbit farming a wonderful experience.
