About Us

Adeiza rabbitry has been on the run for a very long time. It was birthed form the love of Animals and Nature itself. Alongside passion, the business was also birthed in late 2016 so as to provide a source of lively hood and to continually run it with passion and commitment. Adeiza rabbitry was established to generally enlighten Animal lovers on the bases of rabbit farming, this includes provided lectures and articles thrrough social media platforms to generally equip these Farmers for a better backyard or commercial farming. Most importantly it was established to make white meat easily accesible for the Nigerian and African diety system.
